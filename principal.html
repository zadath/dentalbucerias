Smile design

It is a procedure where we develop a prototype to change shape, size and color of your teeth; always looking for the harmony and aesthetics of your Smile.

Veneers and Crowns Porcelain

After making a Smile Design, we proceed to elaborate Emax Porcelain Veneers, as well as Zirconia or Emax crowns.

Dental implants

Implants are a replacement for lost teeth. We must assess the general health of the patient, as well as habits, in addition to their oral health.

Resins

It is an aesthetic restoration that allows obturation of teeth and molars damaged by cavities, cracks and / or fractures. It is placed the color of the tooth. It is used in small and medium fillings. A larger fillings we proceed to place INCRUSTATIONS of which can be totally aesthetic made by Emax porcelain.

Endodontics

If you have a tooth or molar seriously damaged by decay or a serious dental infection (abscess). It is where we could recommend an endodontic treatment. They are used to repair and save your tooth or molar instead of removing it.

Fluoride application

We recommend the application of fluoride to prevent tooth decay and remineralize the enamel of your teeth. Fluorides have the property of reducing the number of caries by more than 50%. For small caries we indicate an application of flour varnish to stop the problem.
